Nice to meet you. In 2020 I launched this blog [to "find a use for the magic"](/en/2020/01/20/daisuke-site-launched/).

> I find everyone as one that has become a wizard. Meanwhile, they also seem like discovering how to use the magic. Certainly, I'm also one of them.

The small magic in our pockets — how can it enrich our everyday lives? Where, with whom, on what, and how do we use that magic?

> Among this, I wonder, "what's priceless for us?" Time to time, I get tempted to stop by and ponder it. Perhaps "this" is also something priceless for us.

As a trail of that never-ending question, I'd like to write here — grounded not in the view of a company or the organizations I belong to, but in my own personal, everyday point of view — about the things I've made, tried, thought, and felt, detours and all.
